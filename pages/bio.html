Biography

Lizhi Chen is currently a Ph.D. student, major in Optical Engineering, advised by Prof. Hao Zhang, in the Department of Precision Instrument at Tsinghua University (THU). Lizhi works in the fields of computational optics, holography and computational displays. He is continuously working on the development of next-generation novel 3D displays and near-eye displays for virtual and augmented reality applications. His recent research is focused on using advanced optical and computational techniques to enhance the visual perception in holographic displays. He received a B.Eng. (Optical Engineering) from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT) in 2018. Outside of work, he enjoys playing tennis, working out, video and traveling.
